Company History

Faridabad's Land Specialists For Over 15 Years

Incorporated in 2005 in Faridabad, Haryana, Homage Infratech is a certified real estate consultancy specializing in plotted developments across residential, commercial, and industrial property segments.

We do not sell built flats, houses, or villas. Our singular focus is land and plots — empowering individuals and commercial investors to purchase clear-titled properties, design their structures, and build their own futures.

"Our mission is to provide accurate, honest, and timely advice to help clients make confident property decisions in Faridabad's fast-changing market."

With deep local roots in A Block, Sector 85, our advisors bridge the gap between complex zoning regulations, legal verifications, and buyers' requirements.

Why Partner With Us?

  • No Flats, No Villas: Sharp focus on plotted developments guarantees specialized knowledge on land titles and zoning laws.
  • Legal Due Diligence: Complete screening of ownership records, registry history, and RERA compliance guidance.
  • End-to-End Support: Assisting buyers from initial layouts and pricing evaluations to registry processes and site handovers.
